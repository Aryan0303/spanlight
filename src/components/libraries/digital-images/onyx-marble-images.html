Onyx and Marble Image Library

Spanlite’s extensive library of digital stone images includes onyx, marble and exotic pieces from around the globe. Suitable for use with our Stratum Image Glass, these images offer the perfect alternative to using real stone slabs.

Photographed by our experts, these image are extremely high resolution, allowing them to be enlarged without loss of detail or definition. Our studio team is on hand to manipulate these images to suit your colour scheme, scale and design requirements.
