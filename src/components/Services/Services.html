About Us

Spanlite initially launched with a single, innovative lighting product, LED Light Sheet.

Developed by founder Adrian Bowker, this unique lighting solution solved many backlighting issues and was quickly adopted by architects, designers and contractors.

As Spanlite's client base and project experience grew, our collaborative approach helped us absorb our clients' visions and challenges, and our creative spirit led us to develop a unique range of effective solutions that are perfectly suited to creating bespoke architectural illuminated surfaces that are easy to implement.

Today we offer a comprehensive set of made-to-measure lighting solutions and translucent surfaces to customers around the world, and our supportive project services help guide our clients through every stage of the design-build process.

Our Services

Our team of designers and engineers are recognised for their unique ability to combine lighting, surface & structure, de livering them seamlessly into the built environment.

We love developing our client's visions into stunning features that exceed their expectations. From design concept through to installation, our team's unique skill set will help fine-tune your project and help you deliver something truly remarkable.

Sales & Client Services

Our friendly sales and client services team will oversee your project through every stage. From your initial enquiry, through design development, production and installation, they are always on hand to help you and will guide y our project to completion. Their proactive stance ensures that each phase is closely monitored and that documents, designs, samples and products are delivered to schedule.

Technical Team

With years of experience in the industry, our technical team are masters at identifying the best systems and bespoke solutions on any project.

Studio & Artwork

Our Design Studio is equipped to handle all your graphic design needs.

Our skilled designers are experts at handling large format imagery and understand how designs can be applied to illuminated surface materials.

Manufacturing

Once all details have been agreed and signed off, your project moves into production with Spanlite’s Manufacturing team.

Everything we make is made-to-measure. Our engineers fabricate each element to a precise set of criteria to match your requirements.

On more unusual projects, samples & mock-ups will be manufactured for approval, validating design decisions and ensuring client satisfaction.

Our Process

1

Consultation
During early consultations, we'll absorb your vision, clarify your overall design intent and identify all practical considerations such as your build environment, timelines and budgets.

2

Project Analysis
Our technical and design teams collaborate to devise the most suitable systems, define materials and establish structural engineering details.

3

Proposal
Our client services team offer our proposal and work with you to ensure that it includes everything you need. Any final details are agreed and our sales team prepare your quotation.

4

Tender Process & Accreditation
Some larger projects will go out to tender with contractors or may require us to go through an accreditation process. Client Services will happily liaise with your affiliates and provide all the required information on time.

5

Production
When you're ready to move forward, our Production Team oversees the manufacture of every element, within the agreed lead time. All of our lighting is manufactured in-house by our experts and we collaborate with trusted partners to deliver a wide range of surfaces and structural components.

6

Delivery & Installation.
We'll arrange for your order to be safely delivered to your preferred location, anywhere in the world.
If you'd like our help on site, our Installation Team can take care of the build for you. They can install the lighting, surface and structural elements and can programme any lighting sequences required for your project. Our installers are respectful, efficient and tidy, and have been trained to industry standards.
