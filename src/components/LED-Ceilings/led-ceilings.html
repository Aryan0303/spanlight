LED Ceilings

Our experts provide 3 of the best solutions for illuminated Ceilings.

There are several options when it comes to LED ceilings and if you’ve got a large ceiling area to cover with an expanse of even light, it can be hard to know what the best solution is.

We’ve put together an objective comparison of the three main contenders >– backlit acrylic panels, backlit fabric panels and backlit stretch ceilings – highlighting their design appeal, merits and limitations.

This article highlights the key similarities and differences between each product.

Diving a bit deeper into each product, let’s first look at what they all have in common:
  • Made to measure, bespoke to your dimensions
  • Even illumination and perfectly diffused light with no hotspots,striping, pin pricks of visible LEDs or shadowing
  • Typical Colour Rendering Index of 85 CRI as standard, with90 CRI+ also available
  • Available in a variety of temperatures and colours:White 2700K – 6500K • Tuneable White 2200K – 6500K • RGBW
  • Reinforced collared cut-outs can be added to allow for ceilingfixtures & existing structures such as wayfinding devices,sprinklers & smoke detectors
  • Clean installation
  • Choice of 1-10 dimmable, DALI, TRIAC & DMX drivers andcompatible w/ standard controllers – Lutron, Creston, Control4 etc
  • Emergency lighting functionality can be integrated into thelighting panels
  • Each option is also available for wall mounting, meaning you cantie wall and ceiling lighting features together
  • Guaranteed to run for 50,000 hours
Now that you’ve ticked these things off your list, it’s time to look at what makes each solution for LED ceilings different.
